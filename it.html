IT in Manchester

Everythingtech

Originally from Manchester itself, Everythingtech has been around for 10 years. Being locally centered in Manchester allows the company to be more hands-on with their clients, 75% of which actually live in Manchester. Working for Everythingtech means close to home as well as working for your community. The team at Everythingtech is small but that makes for a great environment to work and grow with others.

Iih Global

Iih Global was founded in 2013, and has a base in Manchester. They are an award winning company that specializes in software development. Some of their services include web and mobile development, as well as company branding. Iih Global has it own core values which make it a very good place to work due to the mission and the culture that is being implemented at the company. Their core values include growth, work effiency, and being customer centric.

Wenso

Although Wenso has their headquarters in Manchester, they also haves bases in the US, the United Arab Emirates, Canada and India. Their services include web development, support, and analytics. Wenso has their own principles called ACT. This acronym stands for Add business value,Cost optimization, and Time to market acceleration. ACT, along with the company's core values, make Wenso a great place to work in Manchester.
