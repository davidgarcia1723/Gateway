Things to do in Manchester

Check out Old Trafford!

Price ranges from $25-40

Known as the "Theatre of Dreams," Old Trafford is home to Manchester United Football club. Old Trafford offers tours of the stadium and the museum. There is also a Megastore in which people can purchase exclusive merchandise from the team. This activity is great for people of all ages.

Visit the Manchester Museum

Admission is FREE!

The Manchester Musuem is home to exhibitions that range from artifacts and fossils, to nature and culture from around the world. There are drop-in tours available for all the galleries. This activity is suitable for people of all ages.

Drink with the Manchester Distillery

Prices range from $55-120

If you have ever wanted to create your own vodka, then the City of Manchester Distillery is the place for you. This location offers visitors a chance to create their own liquor, while enjoying some liquor. Needless to say, this activity is strictly for adults.
